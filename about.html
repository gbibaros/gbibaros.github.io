Ordained on July 26, 1993, GBI Baros stems from a fellowship at the printing company Palnerindo Cerdas Kasih belongs to Rev. Bonar Simangungsong, led by Johannes Marbun (late) and Janner Simangungsong.

With the increase of the church and the church's desires together, then building procurement committee was formed and God gives a 3 storey building in the area of Pondok Labu. This place was named Kapel Pola (Kantor Pelayanan Pondok Labu).

Two years later, Kapel Pola was opened as a place of worship as well as the secretariat office of the church. Our hope, Kapel Pola can be a blessing for the region of Pondok Labu and a fields to reach souls for the glory of the Lord Jesus.
